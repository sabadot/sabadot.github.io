Who am I?

Hi! My name is Tristan Sabado. I am an undergraduate at York University and Sheridan College for the YSDN program. My body of work includes both printed and time-based projects. Most of my work is currently made up of print projects made for school however I have a particular interest in motion graphics animation. Between editorial and motion design, I am able to use my skills to create narratives out of my work that communicate the ideas that are being presented. I like thinking about how information is communicated over time and I would like to do that myself in my work!
