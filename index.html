Who am I?

My name is Tristan Sabado. I am a 2022 graduate of the York/Sheridan Program in Design (YSDN). I am a graphic designer living in Toronto with a focus on motion and editorial design. Using design as a process, I create videos, books, brand identities, and many other visual objects. Through my process of research, iteration and discussion, I create work that is authentic to your vision. It is a skill cultivated since childhood through curiosity and the critical analysis of how things work. I am the type of person who thrives off people that have stories and ideas they are passionate about. Listening and transforming them into something greater is a privilege that pushes me to keep learning and growing. With my detail-oriented work ethic and disposition to collaborate, I will make sure the experience of creating meaningful things will be inviting and engaging.

Please email me at thetadsn846@gmail.com if you would like to contact me.
