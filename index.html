Who am I?

Hi! My name is Tristan Sabado. I am an undergraduate at York University and Sheridan College for the YSDN program. My body of work includes both printed and time-based projects. Most of my work is currently made up of print projects made for school however I have a particular interest in motion graphics animation. Between editorial and motion design, I am able to use my skills to create narratives out of my work that communicate the ideas that are being presented. Thinking about how information is communicated across peroid of time is fascinating. I also take pride in my hobbies and experiences with video gaming and anime. I like taking inspiration for them as well as learning about Japanese culture. That also inspires me to open my eyes to more cultures and learn about them.
