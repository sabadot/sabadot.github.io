I'm at Soup Motion Typography

Designing Words in Motion

This is the first full motion graphics project I've ever worked on for a high school project. Ironically, despite my lack of skill in After Effects at the time, I look back on this project and see words that are more designed and meaningful than a lot of the recent motion work I've done. The words had a personality to them that I want to bring back and apply to future work. It will be a reminder that regardless of the skill and aesthetic sense I develop, I must not forget that design should precede the style.
