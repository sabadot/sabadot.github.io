Film Teaser Exercise

Practicing Practicality

This is a single exercise I did from a set of eight I did to try out specific things in After Effects. At the time, the movie I referenced in the teaser released and I wanted to try and apply my knowledge in a practical way, unlike my previous motion graphics work. For this exercise, I wanted to try my hand at atmosphere though moving lights, particles, and applying a visual effect to an illustration. This was also a project in quieter and slower communication rather than the action packed movement and constant flow of words used in the lyric typography of my older projects.
